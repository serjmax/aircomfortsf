Oven, Range, Cooktop repair price range

The average price of repairing an oven comprises $220, varying from $125 to $430. It depends primarily on the type of appliance.

Typical problems

During the service life of these appliances, you can expect the following problems to occur:

  • Bad switch that causes the burner to run too hot
  • Broken burner
  • Defective indicator light
  • Stuck oven door
  • Problems with the igniter or valve causing the failure or malfunctioning of a unit
  • Poor heating caused by problems with the main controller or igniter
  • Faulty thermostat or sensor showing incorrect temperatures
