How much soes it cost to fix a fridge or a freezer?

The cost of repairing these appliances varies from $125 to $850, depending on the parts that you need to replace. For instance, a new thermostat is worth at least $125, compared to a new compressor the price of which can be as high as $450.

If you fridge makes loud noises like buzzing or has stopped cooling, it might require repair. General problems include:

  • Annoying noises might be because of the overheating fan
  • Clicking sounds indicate problems with the start relay
  • Poor cooling may be a sign of a faulty compressor
  • And various odd sounds that might signal faults with the icemaker, defroster motor, or the freezer evaporator fan
